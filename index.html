Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

Present value of future cash flows:

\[ PV = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \]

  • \( CF_t \): Cash flow at time \(t\)
  • \( r \): Discount rate
  • \( T \): Number of periods

One-period example: \[ FV = PV \times (1 + r) \]
