Valuation & Discounting

Present value of future cash flows:

\[ PV = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \]

Where:

  • \(CF_t\): Cash flow at time \(t\)
  • \(r\): Discount rate
  • \(T\): Number of periods

Example for a one-period investment: \[ FV = PV \times (1 + r) \]
